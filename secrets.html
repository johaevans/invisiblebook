Invisible Book

In normal light this beautiful handmade book is filled with apparently blank pages. But hidden on the pages and only visible with black light are the secrets that some of you shared here.

The finished book is on display at the SFMOMA library and the Donna Seager Gallery. It has also been part of the following exhibitions: The Art of The Book: Bay Area Treasures at the Donna Seager Gallery in San Rafael, California between March and April of 2006, Made By Hand: An Exhibition of Handmade Books & Ephemera at the W.S. Hoole Special Collections Library in Tuscaloosa, Alabama between January and February of 2006, Inaugural Exhibition, Donna Seager Gallery in San Rafael, California in November of 2005, and the LoopArts Book Show at the Academy of Art University Gallery in March, 2005.

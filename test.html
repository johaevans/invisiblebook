Invisible Book

If you reach this blog it is because I thought you might have some juicy secrets to share with me. I am working on a collaborative art project creating a book with invisible writing that can only be seen with a black light. When seen in a regular environment it will look like a beautifully inconspicuous handmade book.

The finished book will be displayed in an upcoming book-arts show in San Francisco.

Anything you post here will remain absolutely anonymous; even I will not be able to tell who wrote what. The deepest, most explosive, most moving and funniest secrets may find their way into the book.

Please post a comment and select to be anonymous-unless you really don't mind your identity being attached to your secret.

Invisible Book

Please share a secret!—Take part in a collaborative art project to create a book with secret text, revealed only under black light.

In normal light this beautiful handmade book will be filled with apparently blank pages. But hidden on the pages may be the secrets that you share here.

The finished book will be displayed in an upcoming book-arts show in San Francisco, the details of which will be posted on this site in early 2005.

Any secret you submit will remain absolutely anonymous; no one will be able to tell who wrote what. No personal information is requested. Nothing about your connection here is stored.

Your deepest, most explosive, most moving or funniest secrets may find their way into the book. Please share a secret!
